The Easiest Way to Implement 2FA

We recently implemented 2FA for our customers. An evaluation of the popular two factor authentication services made us realize that ease of development for us came at the price of proprietary mobile applications for end users. We believe end users should have a choice which mobile app they use and we want it to be easy for developers to deliver 2FA. This is why we built Authful.

Features

Support for multiple mobile apps

SMS integration

Fallback numbers and recovery codes

For more information and documentation, check out the project on Github.
